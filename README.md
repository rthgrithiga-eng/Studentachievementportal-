objectives:
The main objective of the Student Achievement Portal is to provide a centralized platform for managing and showcasing students' academic, extracurricular, and personal achievements. The system enables students to upload and maintain records of their achievements, while faculty members can verify and monitor them efficiently. It helps educational institutions keep accurate achievement data, generate reports, recognize student accomplishments, and encourage overall student development. The portal reduces manual paperwork and ensures easy access to achievement records whenever required.
Problem Statement:
In many educational institutions, student achievements are recorded manually through paper documents, spreadsheets, or scattered digital files. This makes it difficult to track, verify, and manage achievement records efficiently. Students may face challenges in maintaining a complete record of their accomplishments, while faculty and administrators spend significant time collecting and organizing data.
